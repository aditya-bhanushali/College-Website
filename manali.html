Solang Valley

Solang Valley,
a splendid valley located in Himachal Pradesh,
is known for its beautiful views of snow-capped mountains and adventurous activities like paragliding and skiing.

Rohtang Pass

Rohtang Pass, located at an altitude of 3,978 meters,
is a stunning high mountain pass connecting Manali to the Lahaul
and Spiti valleys of Himachal Pradesh.
